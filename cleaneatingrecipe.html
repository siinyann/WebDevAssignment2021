Southwestern Sweet Potato and Egg Hash

Recipe Summary

Active Time: 15 Mins
Total Time: 30 Mins
Yield: Serves 1 (serving size: about 2 cups)

Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cup (1/2-in.) diced peeled sweet potato
  2. 2 teaspoons water 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil, divided
  3. 2/3 cup chopped red bell pepper
  4. 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  5. 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
  7. 1/4 cup unsalted canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  8. 1 large egg
  9. 2 tablespoons Green Goddess Avocado Sauce
  10. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

Step 1

Place potatoes and 2 teaspoons water in a microwave-safe dish; cover with plastic wrap. Microwave at high until tender, about 4 minutes. Place potatoes on a paper towel–lined plate. Let stand 5 minutes.

Step 2

Heat 1 teaspoon oil in a cast-iron skillet over medium-high. Add potatoes, bell pepper, chili powder, salt, and cumin; cook until potatoes are crisp, 6 to 8 minutes. Stir in black beans; transfer to a plate.

Step 3

Reduce heat to medium. Add remaining 1/2 teaspoon oil to pan. Crack egg into pan; cook until whites are set, 3 to 4 minutes. Place egg on potato mixture. Top with Green Goddess Sauce and cilantro.

Chicken and Vegetable Kebabs With Orzo

Recipe Summary

Active Time: 45 Mins
Total Time: 45 Mins
Yield: Serves 4 (serving size: 1/2 cup orzo, 3 oz. chicken, 1 cup vegetables, and 1 tbsp. sauce)

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  2. 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, divided
  3. 1 tablespoon minced chipotle chiles in adobo sauce plus 1/2 Tbsp. adobo sauce
  4. 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  5. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, divided
  6. 3 (6-oz.) sweet potatoes
  7. 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  8. 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  9. 1 bunch scallions
  10. 2 cups cooked whole-wheat orzo
  11. 1/4 cup sour cream

Directions

Step 1

Toss chicken with 1 tablespoon lime juice, chipotle chiles, adobo sauce, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Let stand 10 minutes. Thread chicken onto 3 (12-inch) metal skewers.

Step 2

Pierce potatoes with a fork. Wrap with damp paper towels, and microwave at high until slightly tender, about 3 minutes. Cut into 1/2-inch rounds. Thread sweet potatoes and bell pepper alternately onto 2 (12-inch) metal skewers.

Step 3

Whisk together 1 tablespoon lime juice, 1 tablespoon oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Brush half of dressing on vegetables.

Step 4

Preheat grill to high (450°F to 550°F). Place chicken on oiled grates; grill, uncovered, until cooked through, 6 to 8 minutes. Grill vegetables, uncovered, brushing with half of remaining dressing, until tender, 4 to 6 minutes. Brush scallions with remaining dressing; grill, uncovered, until charred, 2 to 3 minutes. Chop scallions; toss with orzo, remaining 1 tablespoon oil, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt. Combine sour cream and remaining 1 tablespoon lime juice. Serve with skewers.
