About Us

Healthybites is a health-based company that helps users to develop healthy habits and lead a healthy lifestyle by educating users on the importance of healthy eating. Through the Healthybites website, users can learn about the different types of food and different types of nutrients. We provide different resources such as videos and recipes to educate users about the different types of diets and recipes.We also have a newsletter where users can sign up to receive daily tips of healthy eating.

Diets

Users will be able to search up and learn more about the different types of diets that you can try. From low-carb and low fat- diets to plant-based diets, there are many diet options users can choose from and try them out.

Recipe

Users will also be able to read up on the recipes for the respectives diets. Here users can find recipes from different cuisines from western to asian cuisines.

Nutrition

Users can learn more about nutrition such as macronutrients (which consists of carbohydrates,fats,protein,fibre and water), and micronutrients(which consists of vitamins, minerals and antioxidants)
