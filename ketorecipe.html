Rib-eyes with Veggies and Blue Cheese Butter

Recipe Summary

Active: 10 mins
Total: 25 mins
Yield: Serves 4

Ingredients

  1. 1 ½ pounds fresh green beans, trimmed
  2. 2 cups multi-colored grape tomatoes
  3. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  4. 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  5. 1 teaspoon black pepper
  6. 2 (12 to 16-oz.) boneless rib-eye steaks (about 1 1/4-in. thick)
  7. 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  8. 1 ½ tablespoons crumbled blue cheese
  9. ½ teaspoon chopped fresh thyme

Directions

Step 1

Preheat broiler with oven rack 6 to 8 inches from heat. Place green beans and tomatoes on a large baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of the salt and 1/2 teaspoon of the pepper; toss well.

Step 2

Broil for 5 minutes. Remove baking sheet from oven. Sprinkle steaks with remaining 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Arrange steaks on baking sheet in a single layer, moving green beans and tomatoes aside to make room.

Step 3

Return baking sheet to oven; broil until steaks are desired degree of doneness, 2 to 4 minutes per side, for medium. Remove from oven; let steaks rest 5 minutes before slicing.

Step 4

Combine butter, blue cheese, and thyme in a small bowl; stir well. Serve butter mixture over steaks.

Instant Pot Peruvian Chicken

Recipe Summary

Active: 20 mins
Total: 2 hrs 45 mins
Yield: Serves 4 (serving size: 1 quarter chicken)

Ingredients

CHICKEN
  1. 1 teaspoon lime zest, plus 2 Tbsp. fresh lime juice (from 1 lime)
  2. 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  3. 3 minced garlic cloves (about 3 tsp.)
  4. 2 teaspoons paprika
  5. 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. ½ teaspoon black pepper
  7. ¼ cup canola oil
  8. 1 (3 lb.) whole chicken
GREEN SAUCE (Makes 1 cup)
  1. 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves and stems
  2. 3 jalapeños, seeded and chopped
  3. 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  4. 1 ounce feta cheese, crumbled (about 1/4 cup)
  5. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves
  6. 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice (from 1 lime)
  7. ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  8. ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil

Directions

Step 1

Prepare the Chicken: Stir together lime zest, lime juice, cumin, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper in a medium mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in oil until combined. Set aside. Loosen skin of chicken, and rub mixture under skin and all over top of skin. Transfer to a large bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and chill 2 to 6 hours.

Step 2

Add 1/2 cup water to bottom of an Instant Pot or electric pressure cooker. Insert a rack; place chicken, breast-side up, on rack. Cover with lid; set to HIGH pressure, and cook for 15 minutes.

Step 3

Allow cooker to naturally release steam by turning off and leaving covered for 10 minutes. Remove lid; transfer chicken to a baking sheet lined with a rack. Let chicken rest at room temperature for 5 minutes Turn broiler on HIGH with rack in the middle of oven. Broil until golden brown, about 5 minutes.

Step 4

Prepare the Green Sauce: While chicken cooks, place cilantro, jalapeños, garlic, feta, basil, lime juice, and salt in a food processor, and pulse until well chopped. Slowly add oil while processor is running, and blend until well combined and bright green. Serve with chicken.
