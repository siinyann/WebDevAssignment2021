Delicious beef & cauliflower pie

Recipe Summary

Calories: 320
Serving: 1

Ingredients

  1. 7 oz cauliflower florets
  2. 2 tbsp milk
  3. 1 tbsp cream cheese
  4. salt and black pepper
  5. 1/4 medium red onion
  6. 1 tsp olive oil
  7. 1/4 medium carrot
  8. 1 garlic
  9. 4 oz ground lean beef
  10. 1 oz frozen peas
  11. 1 oz frozen corn
  12. parsley

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°. Add cauliflower florets in boiled water and cook until tender, 10 minutes. Drain well, and mash with a potato masher until smooth. Stir in cream cheese and milk and season with salt and pepper and mash until completely combined and creamy. Set aside.

Step 2

In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion, carrots, and garlic and cook until softened, 5 minutes. Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink, 5 minutes more. Stir in frozen peas and corn and cook until warmed through, 3 minutes more. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3

Transfer the mixture to oven glass dish and top with an even layer of mashed cauliflower and bake 20 minutes or until is golden. Garnish with parsley and serve.

veggie spinach rolls

Recipe Summary

Calories: 60
Serving: 5

Ingredients

  1. 8 oz frozen spinach leaves
  2. 3 egg whites
  3. 1 egg
  4. 1/4 low fat mozzarella cheese
  5. salt and black pepper
  6. 1 clove garlic
  7. 1 tsp olive oil
  8. 1/2 onion
  9. 1/2 red bell pepper
  10. 1 medium carrot shredded
  11. 1.5 tbsp parsley

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 400° F (200° C).

Step 2

Thaw the spinach and squeeze out the water.

Step 3

Mix spinach, 3 egg whites, mozzarella, half the salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl.

Step 4

Place parchment paper on a baking sheet and spray with cooking spray. Move the spinach mixture to the sheet and press it flat, about 8x12 inches in size and roughly ½ an inch thick.

Step 5

Bake for 15 minutes. When done, set aside to cool on a rack.

Step 6

Finely chop onion and parsley. Grate the carrots.

Step 7

Fry the onions and garlic in a skillet with oil. Add carrots and parsley and let it simmer for about 2-3 min. add the other half of the salt, and pepper and mix briefly.

Step 8

Flip the spinach mat and take the skillet off the heat and add an egg. Mix it all together and spread the filling over the now cool spinach mat.

Step 9

Roll up the spinach mat and filling. Bake for 25 minutes.

Step 10

Cut it into 5 slices.
