Vegetarian Recipes

Veggie wrap

Recipe summary

Calories: 260
Serving: 1

Ingredients

  1. 1 medium carrot
  2. 1/4 medium red bell pepper
  3. 1/4 medium red onion
  4. 1 tbsp cottage cheese
  5. 1 tbsp greek yogurt
  6. 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  7. 1 whole wheat tortilla wrap
  8. 1 oz spinach
  9. 1 tbsp pumkin seeds
  10. salt and black pepper

Directions

Step 1

Peel and grate the carrot and cut the the bell pepper into strips

Step 2

Peel the onion and cut in thin rings.

Step 3

In a small bowl dd the cottage cheese, Greek yogurt , and dijon mustard and mix it well.

Step 4

Spread the dressing mixture onto the wraps. Lay out the spinach leaves on top.

Step 5

Put the carrots, bell pepper, onions and sprinkle the pumkin seeds on top.

Step 6

Season with a dash of salt and pepper and fold the wraps a little on both sides.

Step 7

Cut the wraps into halves and serve. Enjoy!

Strawberry spinach and quinoa salad

Recipe Summary

Calories: 290
Serving: 1

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup quinoa
  2. 2 oz spinach
  3. 3 strawberries
  4. 1 tbsp chopped walnuts
  5. 1 tsp lemon juice

Directions

Step 1

In a small saucepan, cook quinoa according to package.

Step 2

Remove from heat fluff and let cool in a bowl.

Step 3

In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and serve.
