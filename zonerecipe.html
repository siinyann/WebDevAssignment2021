Chicken Chow Mein

Recipe Summary

Prep Time (mins): 30
Yield: Serves 4

Ingredients

  1. 14 oz Ground chicken breast
  2. 2 1/2 tbsps Dr. Sears' Zone Olive Oil (divided)
  3. 1 cup Mushrooms (sliced)
  4. 4 oz Lemon grass (minced)
  5. 1 (8 oz) can Water chestnuts (chopped)
  6. 1 tbsp Jalapeno pepper (minced (don't use seeds unless you like it hot))
  7. 1 cup Scallions (minced)
  8. 2 tbsps Fresh cilantro (minced)
  9. 2 tbsps Hoisin sauce
  10. 3 cups Spaghetti squash (cooked)
  11. 1 cup Mandarin orange sections (in water)

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the spaghetti squash as instructed below.

Step 2

Cook ground chicken in 1 tablespoon of olive oil.

Step 3

When the chicken is mostly cooked, add mushrooms, lemon grass, water chestnuts, jalapeno, scallions, and cilantro.

Step 4

Stir in hoisin and saut√© the mixture until the vegetables are cooked to your liking.

Step 5

Place this mixture on a bed of warmed spaghetti squash.

Step 6

Serve 1/4 cup of mandarin-orange sections to each bowl/plate or have it for dessert.

Note:

Step 1

Cook spaghetti squash in the microwave oven.

Step 2

Carefully pierce the squash 6 or 7 times. Cook on high for 10-12 minutes, turning half way.

Step 3

Cook until a fork inserts easily through the skin and into the flesh of the squash.

Step 4

Repeat with 2-minute increments until you can insert the fork.

Step 5

Let stand at least 5 minutes before cutting in half.

Step 6

The squash will be HOT so hold it in a towel or wear gloves when cutting it in half.

Step 7

Scoop out the seeds and guts with a large spoon. Discard.

Step 8

Use a fork to really separate the strands and scrape the flesh out of the shell.

Chicken Zoneitorri

Recipe Summary

Prep Time (mins): 30
Yield: 1

Ingredients

  1. 3 oz Boneless skinless chicken breast - cut into strips
  2. 1 tsp Olive oil
  3. 2 tbsps Kitchen Basics unsalted chicken stock
  4. 1/2 Green bell pepper - large, chopped
  5. 1/2 Red bell pepper - medium, chopped
  6. 1 Onion - medium, coarsely chopped
  7. 2 cups Mushrooms - sliced
  8. 2 cloves Garlic - chopped
  9. 1 cup Diced tomatoes - drained
  10. 3 tbsps Dry white wine
  11. 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  12. 1 cup Cauliflower-mash (Faux Mashed Potatoes)
  13. 1/2 tsp Dr. Sears' Zone Extra Virgin Olive Oil - drizzle

Directions

Step 1

Lightly salt and pepper the chicken pieces.

Step 2

Brown chicken in olive oil on medium-low heat for 3 to 4 minutes on each side in a large skillet.

Step 3

Remove from pan, cover and keep warm.

Step 4

Add chicken stock, peppers and onions and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.

Step 5

Add mushrooms and garlic and continue cooking another 5 to 8 minutes.

Step 6

Add the tomatoes, wine, and Italian seasoning.

Step 7

Place chicken pieces back in skillet, cover and simmer about 15 minutes, turning once.

Step 8

Serve chicken pieces with vegetables over olive oil drizzled Faux Mashed potatoes.
